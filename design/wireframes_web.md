# Web Wireframe Concepts

These wireframes capture the spirit of Constellation:
clean, spacious, elegant, and emotionally calm.

## Group Home
- Header: Group name, photo, member count.
- Monthly Snapshot: mood, themes, standout moment.
- Primary buttons: 
  • My Prompts  
  • Group Responses  
  • Photo Gallery  
  • Monthly Wrapped

## Reflection Submission
- 4 stacked questions.
- Large writing field.
- Photo upload.
- Music link.
- Mood slider.
- Submit and autosave.

## Personal Insights
- Mood over time.
- Thematic clusters.
- My photo timeline.
- My values map.
- Monthly takeaways.

## Monthly Wrapped
- Hero photo.
- Quote highlights.
- Song of the month.
- Sentiment graph.
- Story mosaic.


# Web Wireframes — Constellation
A blend of narrative and structural clarity. 
These are not pixel-perfect screens, but the emotional and functional skeleton of the interface.

The design language:
- spacious  
- quiet  
- confident  
- deliberate  
- moments of glow  

The interface should feel like opening a journal, not an app.
