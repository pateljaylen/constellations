# Constellation Dual-Mode Question Deck
## 40 Questions

Each question is designed to work across two timeframes:
- **Recency:** something from the past few weeks  
- **Timelessness:** a moment from any chapter of your life  

You may answer from whichever timeframe feels most honest today.

---

### SECTION 1 — Decisions, Values & Tradeoffs

1. Think of a moment — recently or from any time in your life — when you didn’t want to show up for someone but either did or didn’t. What drove your decision?

2. Describe a moment — in the past few weeks or from years ago — when you chose comfort over contribution. Why did that choice make sense then?

3. Recall a time — recent or older — when you misunderstood something important. How did you recover, and what did the experience teach you?

4. In a recent busy stretch, or at any point in your life, what was the first thing you deprioritized — and what never gets cut?

5. What’s a belief about relationships, community, or responsibility that you’ve held recently — or always — even if others disagree?

6. Think of a small decision — either this month or from the past — that reflected a deeper value of yours. What was it?

7. In a recent challenge or one from the past, what was your instinct: fix the problem, fix the people, or wait? What does that reveal about you?

8. Describe a moment — lately or from earlier in life — when you realized you were giving too much or not giving enough. What shifted afterward?

---

### SECTION 2 — Autonomy, Identity & Internal Compass

9. In a recent chaotic moment — or one from your past — did you tighten structure or loosen it? How did you learn that about yourself?

10. Think of a time — recently or long ago — when you had to decide between clarity and speed. Which did you choose and why?

11. Recently or earlier in life, when have people misunderstood you at first? What’s the truth underneath that impression?

12. Tell a story — from the past month or any chapter of your life — that explains why you are the way you are today.

13. What boundary have you strengthened recently — or at some pivotal moment in your past — and what prompted that change?

14. What’s something you used to believe about yourself that you no longer believe? Did that shift happen recently or long ago?

15. Describe a moment — this month or from another season of life — when you acted differently than your usual pattern. What caused the deviation?

---

### SECTION 3 — Relationships, Connection & Showing Up

16. Think of a moment — recent or older — when you opened up to someone unexpectedly. What made that possible?

17. Tell a story — from this month or anywhere in your life — about a time you stepped up without being asked. What made you feel responsible?

18. What’s one uncomfortable thing you did — recently or in the past — for the sake of someone you care about?

19. When did you last feel resentment in a group — this month or at some earlier time? What expectation sat underneath that feeling?

20. Think of a recent conflict or one from years ago. What did it teach you about your conflict style?

21. Describe a moment — recent or from any stage of life — when you felt truly seen. What made that moment stand out?

22. Recently or historically, how do you decide whether someone is worth deeper investment in your life?

---

### SECTION 4 — Growth, Learning & Adaptation

23. Describe a time — recently or earlier in life — when you learned something faster than expected. What allowed that acceleration?

24. Describe a moment — this month or from the past — when learning something took longer than expected. What made it challenging?

25. What’s the last meaningful thing you changed your mind about? Did the shift happen recently or gradually over time?

26. Think of a moment — from the past few weeks or much earlier — when you tried something new and weren’t good at it. What happened next?

27. What’s the most surprising thing you’ve learned about yourself — either recently or in the last few years?

28. What’s a skill or trait you’re actively reshaping — now or during a past chapter — and why does it matter to you?

---

### SECTION 5 — Purpose, Ambition & Fulfillment

29. When you imagine your ideal year — now or at a time you once lived — what are you optimizing for, and why?

30. Think of the happiest period of your life — recent or distant. What conditions made it possible?

31. What’s a long-term goal you’ve been circling — now or historically — but haven't acted on yet? What’s holding you back?

32. What’s something you're proud of — recently or privately from your past — that no one else knows about?

33. What are you working toward — now or at another moment in life — that feels meaningful but also intimidating?

34. What do you want future you to thank present you for — this month, this year, or in the long run?

---

### SECTION 6 — Community, Leadership & Shared Life

35. Recently or across your life, how do you decide whether organizing something for a group should fall to you?

36. Describe a moment — recent or from an earlier season — when you strengthened a community around you, intentionally or accidentally.

37. What’s something you quietly contribute — now or habitually in the past — that often goes unnoticed?

38. Think about a time — recent or long ago — when you felt part of something bigger than yourself. What made it meaningful?

39. What role do you wish you played more often in your communities — now or historically — and what stops you?

40. If community were a bank account, what do you tend to deposit and withdraw — recently or throughout your life?
