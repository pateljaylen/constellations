# Constellation

Constellation is a new way to grow with the people who matter.

It transforms small groups (7–25 people) into living, evolving communities through
a shared ritual of monthly reflection, storytelling, and personal insight.

Each member receives 4 high-signal questions on their assigned week.
They respond with stories, photos, music, and a snapshot of their mood.
At month’s end, Constellation gathers everything into a “Spotify Wrapped”-style
mosaic — a collective portrait of who we are becoming.

Constellation is part journal, part community OS, part emotional intelligence
engine. It captures the progression of a life, and the evolution of a group.

This repository contains the product vision, architecture, design foundations,
feature documentation, and initial content required to build the platform.

Constellation is not a social network.
It is a mirror — and a gathering place.
